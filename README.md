## Featured Work

### [Text-Augmented Structural Estimation of Corporate Default](https://github.com/DavidssonDui/Text-Augmented-Reduced-Form-and-Structural-Estimation-of-Corporate-Debt)
Extends Hennessy–Whited (2007) with a text-informed bond pricing channel. Shows that FinBERT 10-K sentiment predicts default in a panel of 63,449 firm-years (top quintile concentrates 86% of subsequent defaults), while Loughran–McDonald dictionary scores do not. Includes a DEQN solver, SMM optimizer, and 218 passing unit tests.
*Python · PyTorch · FinBERT · SEC EDGAR · Compustat/CRSP*

### [The Effect of Public School Lockdown Measures on Test Scores](https://github.com/DavidssonDui/The-Effect-of-Public-School-Lockdown-Measures-on-Test-Scores)
District-level causal analysis of COVID-19 school closures on standardized math achievement. Combines five public datasets (SEDA, CSDH, NCES, NYT, MIT Election Lab) into a 5,504-district panel and applies both two-way fixed-effects OLS and propensity-score-matched DiD. Finds a 0.160 grade-level decline overall, with a disproportionate 0.290 decline for Black students.
*R · plm · MatchIt · Multi-source data integration*

### [Do Student Debt Relief Policies Affect Pre-College Behaviour?](https://github.com/DavidssonDui/Do-Student-Debt-Relief-Policies-affect-Pre-College-Behaviour-)
Evaluates whether New York's 2015 "Get On Your Feet" loan forgiveness program shifted high school graduates' college enrollment decisions. Uses a two-way fixed-effects difference-in-differences design with proxy treatment construction on 907 NYS high schools. Finds evidence of substitution from 2-year to 4-year programs.
*Python · pandas · statsmodels · NYSED administrative data*
